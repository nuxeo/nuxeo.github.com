Releases


VersionRelease date
  • iOS 1.2August, 23rd

    Improvements:
    - Use of a “url” keyboard type for the server URL
    - Improvement for the preview: adding of a spinner and the filename
    - Allow to open a document link while the app is in background

  • iOS 1.1July, 27th

    Improvements:
    - Ability to share documents through messages, mail, Slack…
    - Open your files directly on your mobile application (and share them)
    - On documents listing: actions "Share" and "Add/Remove from Favorites" available by sliding

  • iOS 1.0June, 22nd

    Main features available are:
    - Browse:
    * View thumbnails and access metadata of your documents (creation date, creator name, version, state, contributors)
    * Preview text documents( including notes) and images
    * Play your videos
    - Full text search
    - Manage Favorites: star or unstar any documents
    - Access personal workspace
